here are a list of addons i reccomend and why

most of these link to Firefox's addon store but most of them could also be found on other addon stores.

  • Buster - Solves captchas for you with the power of Wit.ai
  • Dark Reader - Darkens websites that don't have a dark mode
  • uBlock Origin - Blocks ads and trackers.
  • Privacy Badger - Blocks trackers.
  • Decentraleyes - Also blocks trackers.
  • ClearURLs - Removes tracking via URLs.
  • Universal Bypass - Automatically skips annoying link shorteners.
  • Smart Referer - Limits "Referer" header leakage.
  • Bypass Paywalls - Removes paywalls of news sites (Chrome install instructions)
  • Facebook Container - Prevents Facebook from tracking you around the web.
  • I don't care about cookies - Removes cookie warnings from websites.
  • Autoplay No More - Disables autoplaying the next video on video sites automatically.
  • Image Search Options - Makes reverse image searching super easy.
